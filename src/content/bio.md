I am a PhD student in Computer Science at **Nanjing University**, advised by Prof. Qi Fan. Before that, I received my master's degree in Statistics and bachelor's degree in Mathematics and Applied Mathematics from **Nankai University**.

My research focuses on multimodal post-training and evaluation. I work on reinforcement learning for vision-language models and visual generative models, interactive world models, long-video understanding, and evaluation methodologies for multimodal foundation models.

Recent work includes tool-integrated reinforcement learning for long-video understanding, open-world benchmarks for interactive world models, training-free reward modeling for flow editing, and physically-aware camera pose correction for embodied world models.

## Research Interests

- Multimodal post-training and reinforcement learning
- Vision-language models and long-video understanding
- Visual generative models and diffusion RL
- Interactive world models and benchmark design
- Multimodal evaluation and large-scale training systems
